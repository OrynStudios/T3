Meet the man behind the camera

Jonathan Reyes is the heart behind Unforgettable Moments Photography. A visual storyteller with a gift for capturing the fleeting, quiet, and powerful moments that often go unseen. With his trusted Canon in hand, Jonathan doesn’t just take photos. He preserves emotion, memory, and meaning with every frame.
 
Specializing in black and white photography, Jonathan sees the world through a lens of contrast and depth. For him, stripping away color reveals the soul of a moment: the way a smile curves differently when it’s real, the weight of a glance, the quiet echo of joy or longing. In his work, black and white isn’t a filter. It’s a language. His philosophy is simple: every image should make you feel something. 

Shot on Canon

Every image at Unforgettable Moments Photography is captured using Canon’s trusted technology—renowned for its clarity, color depth, and precision. Canon cameras offer a perfect balance of speed and soul. From the soft grain of low light to the crisp edge of a candid smile, Canon helps translate emotion into imagery. Flawless every time.
